INTRODUTION

Established in May 2005 by famous professional teacher Muhammad Bilal Raza Qadri,
Today, we are proud to say that our physical presence extends throughout Karachi with a vast network of 10 branches operating in major areas of Karachi including Kharadar, Garden, Clifton, Jamshed road, North Karachi and Defence area. Our branches are operating under the guidance of a team of over 100 working professionals who educate almost 1000s of students on a daily basis.
